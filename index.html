Importance of Mental Health

for a healthy life starts with a healthy mind

According to the World Health Organization (WHO) more than 264 million people of all ages around the world have some sort of depression. While this number represents the global population, the contributing factors surrounding depression strike a similar chord, from anxiety and drug and alcohol use disorders, to biological issues such as bipolar affective disorder.

Thus, this project aims to:

  1. Foster a better understanding of various mental health challenges
  2. Help make the general public better equiped to help themselves and others through times of distress
  3. Provide easy access to counselling service
  4. Encourage people, and provide them channels, to contribute towards organizations working towards this cause
